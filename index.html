3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information to facilitate app functionalities and enhance user experience:

  • User ID, Name, Email Address, and Phone Number: Collected for account management, authentication, and support.
  • Contacts: Collected for synchronization and sharing features, only with your explicit consent.
  • Text Messages (SMS): We access SMS to read one-time passwords (OTP) for account verification and authentication purposes only.
  • Calendar: Accessed for setting reminders and scheduling meetings, based on your consent.
  • Purchase History: Collected to manage in-app purchases and provide transactional records, with your consent.
  • Device ID and Usage Data: Collected to improve app performance and security.
  • Precise Location Data: Collected to provide location-based services with your consent.
  • Camera and Microphone: Accessed for multimedia features such as video calls or taking photos, with your permission.
  • Biometric Data: Collected for secure authentication (e.g., fingerprint or facial recognition), based on your consent.
  • Address Data: Collected to manage purchases, deliveries, or for service personalization.

3.2 Non-Personal Information

We also collect non-identifiable data such as device type, operating system, and interactions within the app to improve functionality.

We use the data collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain app functionalities.
  • To personalize services and enhance user experience.
  • To process payments securely and manage purchases.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

5. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data

We process data based on:

  • Consent: For access to features such as location services, text messages, and contacts.
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide app services and customer support.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve security, detect fraud, and enhance the app.
  • Legal Compliance: To meet legal obligations, such as regulatory compliance.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Once no longer required, the data is securely deleted.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, anonymized, aggregated data may be shared with trusted partners for analytics and app improvement. All sensitive data is processed via our middleware API, ensuring that actual data is anonymized and secure.

8. Data Security

We have implemented robust security measures, including 256-bit SSL encryption, to protect your personal information. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we ensure that third-party access is controlled and secure.

  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your data has been misused or mishandled.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent at any time where data processing is based on consent.

For users in the EU, you can lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority in your country if you have concerns about how we handle your data.
